I am currently a PhD student in artificial intelligence at the Applied Data Mining research group, Queensland University of Technology, Australia. Projects

  • FarsTail dataset

    FarsTail is the first relatively large-scale Persian dataset for Natural Language Inference (NLI) task with more than 10,000 samples. To ensure the quality of FarsTail, the data preparation process is almost similar to the well-known SciTail dataset, and several steps of cleaning and relabeling have been performed on this dataset. Along with the raw Persian samples, we provide the indexed format of FarsTail to be usable for non-Persian researchers as well.

  • Predicting a banking indicator from news texts

    I applied NLP techniques on crawled news texts of recent years to predict monthly changes in one of the most important indicators of Liquidity Risk Management in Banks.
